# CartX Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Audit Report
**Project:** FunnelIQ
**Date:** May 2026

## 1. Executive Summary
This report analyzes 150,000 user sessions to identify conversion bottlenecks and revenue leakage points across the CartX platform. The overall conversion rate stands at **4.47%**. While this is near the industry average, deep segmentation reveals critical UX friction points, particularly on mobile devices and within specific traffic channels.

## 2. Key Findings

### 2.1 The Mobile Checkout Friction Point
* **Observation:** Mobile traffic accounts for approximately 60% of total sessions but only ~40% of completed purchases.
* **Data Point:** The drop-off rate from `begin_checkout` to `purchase` is significantly higher on Mobile (estimated 65% abandonment) compared to Desktop (estimated 35% abandonment).
* **Implication:** The current mobile checkout experience is actively repelling high-intent buyers, costing an estimated ₹1.2M in lost revenue per month.

### 2.2 Cart Abandonment by Source
* **Observation:** Users arriving from Paid Social campaigns have a high `add_to_cart` rate but the highest cart abandonment rate among all sources.
* **Data Point:** Paid Social drives 30% of cart additions but only 15% of purchases.
* **Implication:** The intent generated by social ads is impulsive. The checkout process is either too long to capture this impulse, or shipping costs/hidden fees revealed at checkout are deterring users.

## 3. Prioritized Recommendations

| Priority | Issue | Actionable Recommendation | Expected Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High** | Mobile Checkout Friction | Implement 1-Click Checkout (Apple Pay, Google Pay). Minimize form fields and use numeric keypad attributes for phone/zip inputs. | +15% Mobile Conv. Rate (Est. +₹1.2M/mo) |
| **High** | Social Traffic Abandonment | Deploy aggressive remarketing campaigns targeting cart abandoners from social sources. Implement exit-intent popups offering temporary free shipping. | +10% Recovery Rate (Est. +₹800k/mo) |
| **Medium** | Product Page Drop-off | Add high-quality video reviews and trust badges near the 'Add to Cart' button to increase product page conversions. | +5% Add-to-Cart Rate |
| **Low** | Guest Checkout Visibility | Ensure the "Checkout as Guest" option is styled as prominently as the login/register options. | Marginal improvement |

## 4. Next Steps
1. **A/B Test 1-Click Checkout:** Launch an A/B test on mobile devices routing 50% of traffic to a streamlined 1-click checkout flow.
2. **Review Checkout Analytics:** Post-implementation, monitor the `begin_checkout` to `purchase` step specifically for the variant group.
3. **Deploy Retargeting:** Sync the "Cart Abandoners - Paid Social" segment to the ad platform for immediate retargeting.
